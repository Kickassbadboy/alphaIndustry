Welcome to Alpha

Precise, reliable and agile tooling solutions.

For over the past 2 decades, Alpha has been able to provide the reliable tooling solutions

We have supplied equipment to prominent industries in varied streams of the industry in India and abroad.

Alpha is recognised for its technical expertise in the critical tool and core making and has proven its calibre time and again.

Having the state of art in-house CAD / CAM Design room and pattern shop, we are able to provide our esteemed customers - foundries and engineering companies with the high quality foundry patterns needed for excellent metal casting.
