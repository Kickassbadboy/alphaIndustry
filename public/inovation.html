About Us Innovation

Innovation

Alpha has always regarded continuous improvement a virtue since inception and we have upgraded our processes by taking valuable feedback from customers and ingenious inputs from research and industry. This has helped us remain at the forefront of the tooling industry and a preferred choice of customers.


With experience gathered over the years from our partnerships with esteemed customers and the learning experiences from challenging projects we have optimised our operations using the Workflow principles to ensure a steady and efficient operation across Alpha.

Each one of us at Alpha follow the workflow guidelines and this helps us to standardise the process and bring out the best of our capabilities.




Customers and our partners have appreciated our efforts in conceptualising this system and our implementation in the daily practice across Alpha. We believe that consistent performance is only possible when you abide by the principles that you have mutually agreed upon and help us excel in the versatile field of foundry tooling and solutions. We thank our customers, stakeholders and employees to have contributed to our effort and have made possible for us to keep on improving our operations and in-turn serve them better.
