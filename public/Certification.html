Certification

Over the years Alpha has stood with foundries and other manufacturers to help them optimise their toolings and processes. Customer feedback is considered an important asset at Alpha and st all instances we have enhanced our processes to suit the customer requirement. This has brought in appreciation from our esteemed customers on the quality and reliability of our product and services.

Alpha has received “Supplier of the Year” awards from multiple esteemed customers as an appreciation of our partnerships and support through the years.This recognition has put Alpha at the lead in the tooling suppliers worldwide and has helped motivate us to keep on innovating and upgrading our systems.

Based on our firm hold in precision, reliability and efficient service, we have earned long-term associations with numerous customers in India and around the world. These partnerships have helped us learn how to handle turnkey projects, develop precise equipment and provide a complete solution to our customers.

Industry-standard quality certifications and professional management systems are in place at Alpha since inception and have helped us achieve an ideal working environment. Safety is regarded as the topmost principle in Alpha’s work culture and we have worked alongside safety experts to create awareness amongst employees and establish safe working habits amongst the workforce.
