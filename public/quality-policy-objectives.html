About Us Quality Policy & Objectives

Quality Policy

Alpha Dies & Pattern (I) Pvt. Ltd. operates in industry sectors which are dynamic and demand stringent quality requirements. Over the recent years while keeping our focus on quality we have attempted to raise the bar of the quality standard that can be achieved. We believe that Quality is our duty and one of the most fundamental


Quality vision 2020

We aspire to be the most trusted and preferred partner to leading product developers over India and support them with our expertise in foundry toolings and dies. Defence and farm equipment will be a focus-sector and we look forward to serving India in these foundational areas. Alpha will also navigate around the globe in search of markets which give an opportunity to work on international projects to bring attention of customers to the superior indian product quality.

Customer Centric Objectives

ALPHADEPEND

On time delivery. Reliability. Always.

In Alpha’s work ethics, Delivery commitment is as respected by all the team members. First-time-okay tool provides the customer with a sense of reliability in operations at their end and customers can always rely on Alpha for any future support.


ALPHAONE

One contact person. Directly. Exactly.

At Alpha each customer is linked with a single point of contact for their project. The person will coordinate all the steps in the complete process. The benefits are discernible : Clear communication, easy decision making and seamless accessibility.
